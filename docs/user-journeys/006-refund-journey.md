# The Refund Request — Escrow Recovery Journey

## Actor
A buyer who is unsatisfied with a delivered service and wants their money back.

## Goal
Initiate a refund request and recover their escrowed funds.

## Steps
1. Navigates to an active or completed contract.
2. Submits a refund request with a reason and supporting evidence.
3. The provider is notified and given a window to respond.
4. If the provider agrees or doesn't respond within the deadline, the escrow is released back to the buyer.

## Pain Points
- Providers who dispute the refund aggressively.
- Unclear refund policy — buyers don't know their rights.
- Slow processing — refund can take days to clear back to the original payment method.

## Success Criteria
The buyer successfully recovers their funds through the escrow refund process.
