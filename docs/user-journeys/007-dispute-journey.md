# The Dispute Resolution — Mediation Journey

## Actor
A buyer or provider who is in a disagreement over a contract's scope, quality, or payment terms.

## Goal
Resolve the dispute fairly through mediation or arbitration.

## Steps
1. One party files a formal dispute on the contract.
2. The platform locks the escrow funds — neither side gets paid until resolution.
3. Both parties submit evidence (messages, deliverables, contract terms).
4. A neutral mediator reviews the case and issues a binding ruling.
5. Escrow is released according to the ruling (full or partial to either side).

## Pain Points
- Bias concerns — parties don't trust the mediation process.
- Evidence disputes — conflicting claims with no clear proof.
- Prolonged resolution — funds are locked for weeks or months.

## Success Criteria
The dispute is resolved within a defined timeframe and funds are released according to the mediator's ruling.
