# The Provider — Service Publishing Journey

## Actor
A new user who wants to offer their skills on the platform. This could be a developer, designer, writer, or organizer.

## Goal
Create a service listing, attract clients, and earn income.

## Steps
1. Creates a profile with skills and portfolio samples.
2. Publishes service listings with pricing and scope.
3. Responds to buyer inquiries and accepts contracts.
4. Delivers work, receives escrow payout (95% to them, 5% to solidarity fund).

## Pain Points
- Standing out in a crowded marketplace.
- Unclear expectations from buyers leading to scope creep.
- Payment delays or disputes.

## Success Criteria
The provider publishes at least one service listing and receives their first contract.
